🏋️ Workout Breakdown
💪 Strength (25 min)

Focus on slow, controlled movements. Build muscle to boost resting metabolism.

Routine: Squats, lunges, push-ups, glute bridges, planks. (Use water bottles if no dumbbells).
🔥 HIIT (20 min)

High Intensity Interval Training. 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest. Repeat.

Routine: Jumping jacks, burpees, high knees, mountain climbers, fast step-ups/stair climbs.
🚴 Cardio (30 min)

Sustained effort to keep the heart rate up and burn calories continuously.

Routine: Skipping / Jumping rope (highly recommended!), Stair climbing, brisk walk, light jog, or dancing.
